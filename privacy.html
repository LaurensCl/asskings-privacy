ASSKINGS Privacy Policy

Last updated 29 April 2026 · Version 1.0

1. Who we are

ASSKINGS ("we", "us", "our") is the data controller for personal data processed through the ASSKINGS mobile application and related services. You can reach us at hello@askings.nl.

This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use and share it, and what rights you have. ASSKINGS is anonymous by design: we collect the minimum we need to run the Service.

2. Data we collect

Account data: your email address (for sign-in via one-time password) and your date of birth (to confirm you are at least 18 years old).

Profile data: an anonymous username you choose, plus optional taste-quiz answers used to personalize your feed.

Content data: photos you post, ratings and feedback you give, comments, and post metadata such as context (date / work / event), city you choose to attach, visibility setting, and timestamp.

Technical data: device identifiers used for push notifications (when you opt in), session tokens, and limited diagnostic information such as crash logs.

We do not collect real names, phone numbers, social-network identifiers, contact lists, precise GPS coordinates beyond the city you choose to share, or biometric data.

3. Legal basis

We process your data on the following GDPR (Regulation (EU) 2016/679) bases: performance of our contract with you (to operate your account and the Service), our legitimate interests (to keep the Service safe, prevent abuse, and improve the product), legal obligation (to retain certain records when required), and your consent (for optional features such as push notifications).

4. How we use your data

To create and manage your account; to authenticate you via OTP; to display your posts and ratings to the audience you choose; to run automated content moderation; to send transactional email such as login codes and account notifications; to detect and prevent abuse; to comply with legal obligations.

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell, rent, or trade your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Who we share data with

We share data only with sub-processors that help us run the Service: Supabase (database, authentication, file storage); Resend (transactional email delivery); Anthropic (automated content moderation, photos shared via temporary signed URLs and not retained by Anthropic for training).

These sub-processors process data on our behalf under written agreements that meet GDPR requirements.

We may disclose data when required by law, valid legal process, or to protect our rights, users, or the public from harm.

6. International transfers

Some of our sub-processors are based outside the European Economic Area, including in the United States. When we transfer your data outside the EEA we rely on appropriate safeguards such as the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses or, where available, adequacy decisions.

7. Retention

We keep account and content data while your account is active. When you delete your account, your profile, posts and ratings are removed within 30 days, except for limited backups retained up to 90 days for disaster-recovery purposes and records we are required to retain by law (such as tax or fraud-prevention records).

Rejected photos are removed from our storage immediately after moderation.

8. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to: access your data; rectify inaccurate data; erase your data ("right to be forgotten"); restrict or object to processing; data portability; withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

You can exercise most of these rights directly in the app: edit your username and profile from Settings, download a copy of your data from Settings → Download my data, delete your account from Settings → Delete Account. For other requests email hello@askings.nl. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Dutch Data Protection Authority (Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens).

9. Security

Data in transit is encrypted using TLS. Data at rest is encrypted by our database and storage providers. Access to user data within ASSKINGS is restricted on a need-to-know basis and protected by per-user row-level security rules at the database layer.

No system is perfectly secure. If we become aware of a personal data breach that is likely to result in a risk to your rights, we will notify you and the relevant supervisory authority as required by law.

10. Children

ASSKINGS is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact hello@askings.nl and we will delete the account.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified through the app and via email at least 14 days before they take effect. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the latest revision.

12. Contact

Questions, requests, or concerns about your data? Reach us at hello@askings.nl.
